Francisco’s Halo Halo

Francisco’s Halo-halo is a Filipino dessert food stall that offers authentic Halo-Halo and various refreshments on-the-go with the aim of providing a taste of the Philippines’ finest fruit preserves mixed in a cup made right in front of its customer. With the presernce of the Filipinos’ sweet tooth, Francisco’s envisions to cater autherntic Filipino dessert that is on-the-go, accessible, and quality filled.
