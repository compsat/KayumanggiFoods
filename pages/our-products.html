Red Mongo

Red Mongo, widely known as Red Mung Beans, is a famous legume dessert ingredient all over Asia. Sweetened with syrup and preserved in can, Red Mongo is often added as a flavoring for bean pastes, a topping for various Asian pastries, biscuits, and delicacies, and a filling for sweetened spring rolls. Partnered with White Beans and Garbanzos, Red Mongo is a primary ingredient of Filipino Halo-halo dessert.

Packed in bottled jars (sizes 12 oz. and 32 oz.) and pouches (500g and 1000g).
